1) Purpose of Processing

We collect and use personal information solely for user registration and account management, including creating and managing an account, verifying membership, and providing in‑game services such as saving and displaying gameplay records.

2) Items of Personal Information

CategoryItemsMethod
User Registration Email address Entered by the user at sign‑up
Gameplay Data Game records (e.g., match history, win/loss, ratings/ELO) Automatically generated during gameplay

We do not use external logins (e.g., Google/Apple Sign‑In).

3) Retention & Deletion

  • We retain personal data only while the account remains active.
  • Upon account deletion or withdrawal of consent, we immediately and permanently delete personal data and gameplay records.

4) Provision to Third Parties

We do not provide personal information to third parties.

5) Outsourcing / Processors

We use external service providers (processors) for limited purposes:

ProcessorPurposeLocationRetentionPolicy
Amazon Web Services (AWS) Server hosting (EC2) Republic of Korea (Seoul region, ap‑northeast‑2) Until account deletion AWS Privacy
Google AdMob (Google LLC) In‑app advertising Global processing (incl. U.S./Asia) Per Google retention Google Privacy
Unity Technologies Game engine platform Global processing Per Unity retention Unity Privacy

6) Children Under 14

The App may be used by children under 14. Where consent is required, we obtain verifiable consent from the legal guardian before collecting or using the child’s personal information. Only minimal information necessary for consent verification (e.g., child’s email and guardian’s contact details) is collected, and the consent status is confirmed via the method we provide.

7) Automatic Collection (Ads/Cookies)

We use AdMob SDK which may collect device or advertising identifiers to deliver and measure ads, and to provide optional personalized ads.

  • Opt‑out of personalized ads:
    • Android: Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization
    • iOS: Settings → Privacy → Tracking → Disable “Allow Apps to Request to Track”
  • We do not use additional tracking cookies beyond AdMob’s default operation.

8) User Rights & Requests

Users may request to access, correct, or delete their personal information, or withdraw consent at any time. Accounts can be deleted via the in‑app/website account settings or by contacting us.

9) International Transfers

Your data is primarily stored on AWS Seoul (ap‑northeast‑2) servers located in the Republic of Korea. AdMob and Unity may process limited data on servers outside Korea, subject to their global privacy frameworks and safeguards.

10) Security Measures

  • Encrypted transport (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Access controls and least‑privilege administration
  • Secure server configuration and regular updates

11) Changes to this Policy

We will announce updates on the Google Play Store listing and update the “Last updated” date at the top of this page.
